🍛 Food — feeding Indian parents in America
Stock up on Indian groceries before Amma and Baba arrive — Apna Bazar or Patel Brothers in Beaverton. Get atta, dal, rice, ghee, pickles, their favourite snacks
Jackson Heights, Queens (NYC) — must visit. Literally feels like India — incredible desi food, Indian shops, Bollywood music everywhere
Fremont + Milpitas (Bay Area) — dense Indian restaurant scene. Saravana Bhavan for south Indian. Vik's Chaat in Berkeley is legendary
Pack thepla, namkeen, Indian snacks for Grand Canyon, Yellowstone, and Big Sur road trips — veg options at national parks are very limited
Las Vegas has Curry Leaf, Rasoi, and Namaste India for pure vegetarian options
🛕 Temples across America
Shiva Vishnu Temple, Livermore CA — one of the most authentic Hindu temples in the US. Major pilgrimage moment for Baba and Amma
Ganesha Temple, Flushing Queens NY — very popular with Indian community, traditional South Indian style
ISKCON temples in almost every major city — Portland, Seattle, San Francisco, NYC, DC. Check local schedules for aarti times
BAPS Swaminarayan temple near Portland (Redmond WA) — worth a visit on a Seattle trip day
🏥 Health + Comfort
Get travel/visitor health insurance immediately — a single US ER visit can cost $5,000+. Get 3-month coverage from visitorcoverage.com
High elevation alert: Grand Canyon South Rim 7,000ft, Yellowstone 6,000–8,000ft, Crater Lake 7,100ft — take it slow the first day at each
Comfortable walking shoes are critical — buy and break in early. Skechers GoWalk or New Balance work great
Bring all prescription medications in original packaging. Keep 1–2 week extra supply
Sunscreen SPF 50+ for all outdoor trips, especially at high elevation — Indian skin often underestimates US sun intensity
🛍️ Shopping — taking gifts back to India
Oregon has NO sales tax — buy electronics, clothes, shoes, gifts here. Best state for shopping!
Woodburn Premium Outlets (45 min south) — Nike, Adidas, Columbia, Coach at outlet prices, zero tax
Costco — take Baba and Amma at least once. The scale of American wholesale is genuinely mind-blowing
Electronics: check voltage compatibility before buying appliances to take back (India 220V, US 110V)
💡 Practical Tips
America the Beautiful Pass ($80) — covers ALL US national parks for a year. Buy on the very first trip
Download Google Maps offline for every destination before leaving Portland
Explain US tipping culture to Baba and Amma — 18–20% at restaurants is standard
Sunset is 9pm in June/July in Portland — long beautiful evenings perfect for after-work sightseeing till 8:30pm
Portland Pickles minor league baseball — quintessential American summer evening, tickets ~$12–18
